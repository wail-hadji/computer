How is the performance of the processor measured?

Clocking rate is the main feature of CPUs when it comes to performance and clocking rate is the basic rate of expressing the number of cycles per second.

It is measured in units of (hertz, kilohertz, megahertz, gigahertz) and time frequency in any synchronous circuit. Every single time cycle (usually less than a nanosecond in modern non-embedded processors) switches between the states of logical zero and logical one.
